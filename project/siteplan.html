Family Budget Planner - Site Plan

Site Name

Family Budget Planner

This name represents a simple and friendly tool for families to organize their monthly income, expenses, and savings. It reflects my goal to help families be more aware of how they use their money.

Optional domain availability: familybudgetplanner.net

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to help families plan their budget by recording their income and expenses in an easy and visual way. The site will include a form to add expenses, display totals, and show a report with categories to help users make better financial decisions. It will be responsive and interactive using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

Scenarios

  • How can I track all my family expenses in one place?
  • Can I see how much I am spending on food or housing each month?

Color Schema

The color scheme will be calm and professional, representing stability and clarity.

  • Main Color: #2E8B57 (Sea Green) – used for headings, buttons, and accents.
  • Secondary Color: #F0F8FF (Alice Blue) – used for backgrounds and light areas.
  • Neutral Color: #333333 (Dark Gray) – used for body text.
